How to make a quesadilla
My name is Hristo Panayotov and this is my personal quesadilla recipe. I hope that it helps you become a better cook.
1. Grab 2 flour tortillas and some shredded cheese (about 1/2 cup). You can also add extras like chicken, veggies, or beans if you want!
2. Heat a pan over medium heat. Butter one side of the first tortilla and place it buttered-side down in the pan.
3. Sprinkle the cheese (and any extras) evenly over the tortilla.
4. Place the second tortilla on top.
5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy, then carefully flip it.
6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the other side is golden and the cheese is melted
7. Remove from the pan, cut into slices, and enjoy your delicious quesadilla!
